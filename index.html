Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, saut√© pancetta with garlic in olive oil until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs with grated Parmesan, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some cooking water.
  5. Mix pasta quickly with pancetta and remove garlic.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and pepper.

Avocado Toast

A quick, healthy, and tasty breakfast or snack option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chili flakes, olive oil drizzle

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices to your liking.
  2. Mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on toast.
  4. Sprinkle chili flakes and drizzle olive oil if desired.
  5. Serve immediately.
